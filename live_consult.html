Our Consulting Services

Our expert consulting services guide MSMEs through the entire ZED journey. We provide handholding support to help you understand the parameters, improve your processes, and achieve your desired certification level (Bronze, Silver, or Gold).

How We Help

  • Gap Analysis: We analyze your current systems against ZED parameters to identify areas for improvement.
  • Documentation Support: Assistance in preparing and organizing the necessary documentation for certification.
  • Process Optimization: Guidance on implementing Zero Defect Zero Effect practices in your manufacturing.
  • Pre-assessment Audits: We conduct mock assessments to ensure you are ready for the final audit.

Why Choose Us?

With our deep understanding of the ZED scheme and experience in industrial consulting, we ensure a smooth and successful certification process for your organization.
